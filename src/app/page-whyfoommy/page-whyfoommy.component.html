Why Foommy?
Foommy is a food sharing network in connecting people who love cooking and appreciate homemade food from all over the world
One of Foommy's aims is to reduce food waste. How much food have you thrown in the trash can lately?
Foommy is an innovation in changing the way people consume food. The more you share, the less you spend
